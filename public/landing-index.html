Your Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about getting started with MediTrack+.

How secure is my patient data?

+

Your patient data is protected with bank-level encryption, multi-factor authentication, and comprehensive audit trails. We're fully HIPAA, GDPR, and SOC 2 compliant with 99.99% uptime guarantee.

Can I use MediTrack+ offline?

+

Yes! Our offline-first architecture ensures you can continue working seamlessly during connectivity issues. All data syncs automatically when you're back online.

How accurate is the AI diagnostics?

+

Our AI achieves 99.7% diagnostic accuracy, trained on millions of medical cases. It serves as a powerful second opinion tool, not a replacement for clinical judgment.

We offer 24/7 technical support, comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and dedicated account managers for enterprise clients.

Absolutely! Our API-first design allows seamless integration with existing EHR systems, labs, pharmacies, and third-party applications.
